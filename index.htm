Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is still under construction, mostly on how much flour you should use so the dough isn't too sticky to portion. Feel free to change this recipe to fit your style and taste!

Ingredients

  • Bread flour
  • All Purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Instant coffee
  • Chocolate Chips

Let's Bake!

  1. Sift 2/3 of a cup of bread flour
  2. Sift 1 cup of all purpose flour
  3. Add 2 tps of salt
  4. Add 2 tps of baking soda
  5. Add 1/2 a cup of brown sugar
  6. Add 1 cup of white sugar
  7. Melt 1 cup of butter
  8. Grab a seperate bowl for wet ingredients
  9. Pour melted butter into seperate bowl
  10. Add 1 tbs of vanilla extract
  11. add 1 tbs of instant coffee to wet ingredients
  12. Add 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk
  13. Mix wet and dry ingredients
  14. Add 1 cup of chocolate chips-- Or as many as you'd like!
  15. Roll dough into 1.5 in balls and squish slightly onto the pan
  16. Bake at 350F in the oven for around 15 minutes

Sidenotes:

If the dough is too sticky, don't panic! Lather your hands in flour and roll them into balls, it'll be fine! Make sure that each cookie has enough room for it to expand withouttouching the others. If you have too many on one pan, it may turn into a cookie cake!
