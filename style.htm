Things You Might Wanna Take To The Beach

Nearly everytime someone plans a trip to the beach, they forget something fairly important. Or at least, something that inconvenices them. Sometimes it's your camera, sometimes its your underwear, whatever it may be! I'm here to help you make sure every single thing is packed!

  1. Clothes for each day you're there
  2. At least half as many PJs
  3. Extra undies
  4. Sunscreen
  5. Sunglasses
  6. Bathing Suit
  7. Sandals
  8. Tank Tops
  9. Shorts
  10. A Hat
  11. Cell Phone & Charger
  12. Snacks for the road!
  13. Toothbrush & Toothpaste
  14. Shampoo & Conditioner
  15. Soap
  16. Beah Towels
  17. Good walking shoes & socks
  18. Camera
  19. headphones
  20. Laptop
  21. Wallet/Purse
  22. First Aid Kit

You may want to pack other things depending on the location and the time of year. You may pack things like diapers and wet wipes, or even female products depending on who is on the trip. I've covered the most basic things that you should not forget on your trip, but make sure you think of everyone when you pack. Making a list a week in advance and adding to it as you go is always a good idea!
